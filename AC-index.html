Magikarp

Although this Pokémon is capable of surviving in even the most polluted ponds, it is usually overlooked by trainers because it is pathetically weak.

  • Pokemon type: Water
  • Region: Kanto
  • Habitat: All bodies of water
  • Evolution: Giyarados

Magikarp is a pathetic excuse for a Pokémon that is only capable of flopping and splashing. This behavior prompted scientists to undertake research into it.
